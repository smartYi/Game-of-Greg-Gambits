Carla is an old teacher with a new attitude. She has been teaching children for three decades (and that means 30 years since a decade is 10 years!*), but Carla has never been old-fashioned. Carla teaches the 3 Rs**. Pick a topic, click on the link, and get started!

**Carla asks: Can you figure out why Reading, Writing, and Arithmetic are called the 3 Rs?

*Carla's Motto: Never miss a chance to teach -- and to learn!

**Answer: Even though Writing and Arithmetic don't start with the letter R, if you say them aloud: Reading, Writing, and 'Rithmetic, they all sound like words that begin with R.
